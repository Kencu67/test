About

Bence is a rising music artist, composer, sound designer, and sample library developer. Born in Hungary and now based in Austria, he is pursuing a full-time career in music. His biggest strength is versatility: from heavy guitars to folk instruments, his palette extends far beyond the traditional Western orchestra. He’s not afraid to explore new territories, using foley, experimental sound design, or even creating custom virtual instruments when the project calls for it. Bence’s focus is always on crafting the exact sound a story needs—no matter how unconventional the path to get there. He works across a wide range of genres and formats, from cinematic scores to electronic tracks and hybrid orchestral pieces.
